About 9D Capital

9D Capital LLC is a registered investment adviser with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (CRD #308524). We operate under a fiduciary standard, meaning we are legally obligated to act in our clients' best interests at all times.

Our firm provides independent, conflict-aware advice across wealth management, family office, and estate planning. Client assets are held in custody at SEI Private Trust Company, a federally chartered limited-purpose savings association, ensuring full separation of advisory and custodial functions.
